Our Mission

At Synergy, our mission is to empower underserved youth by providing access to high-quality STEM education, mentorship, and hands-on experiences. We aim to inspire the next generation of innovators, problem-solvers, and leaders by fostering curiosity, creativity, and collaboration. Through our programs, we equip students with the skills and confidence to thrive in STEM fields and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

About Synergy

Synergy is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering students and transforming communities through STEM education. Our goal is to bridge the gap between students from underserved backgrounds and the resources they need to thrive in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. By providing hands-on workshops, mentorship, and real-world projects, Synergy helps students build the skills and confidence to become the innovators of tomorrow.

Our Purpose

At Synergy, we believe that every student deserves the opportunity to explore their potential in STEM, regardless of their background or circumstances. Our programs are designed to ignite curiosity, foster collaboration, and create a supportive environment where students can engage with complex problems and develop creative solutions. By connecting skilled students with nonprofits, Synergy also facilitates meaningful contributions to the community, helping students realize the impact they can make in the world.

About the Founder

Synergy was founded by Victoria Hong, a passionate advocate for STEM education and a dedicated mentor to young innovators. With a background in electrical and computer engineering, Victoria broke through barriers as a first-generation, low-income Vietnamese woman to pursue her own academic and professional success. Having experienced firsthand the transformative power of education, she established Synergy to create more opportunities for students from underrepresented communities to engage with STEM in a meaningful way.

Victoria's vision for Synergy stems from her belief in the potential of every student to become a leader and problem solver. Through her leadership, Synergy is committed to nurturing that potential by providing the resources, mentorship, and real-world experiences students need to succeed.
