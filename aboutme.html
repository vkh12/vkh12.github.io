About Kieu

I am a Vietnamese product designer who strives to create meaningful work that promotes sustainability and solves real-world problems.

I was born and raised in New Jersey by my Vietnamese immigrant parents and am a first-generation student studying Computer Engineering at Rutgers University.

At this University, I have researched in the Cyber-Physical Systems Lab and Robotics, Automation, & Mechatronics lab where I focused on machine learning and robotics research. While in the RAM lab, I published my first paper as co-author.

When I am not working, you can find me blasting and dancing to EDM songs, binging Netflix shows, and creating new food recipes in my kitchen.
